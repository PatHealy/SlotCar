LowPoly Cars Pack v1.0

This pack contains 16 low poly assets perfect for mobile games and already prefabed for ease of use. Wherever possible, components have been detached to give you full control of wheels, ladders and tow arm components for custom animation purposes.

- Ambulance

- School Bus

- Prison Bus

- Public Bus

- FBI Van

- Firetruck (with detachable ladder for animation purposes)

- Police Car

- Sheriff Car

- NYPD Car

- UK Police Car

- Undercover Police Car (with detachable light for undercover police car)

- Simple Car (with 3 different variations in green, blue and red + psd texture with mask for easy color alterations)

- Taxi

- Empty Truck

- Garbage Truck

- Tow Truck (with detachable components for tow arm)
